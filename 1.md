### What's your understanding for architecture?
---
There're 2 theories to define architecture. The one is composing theory, that's mean that a complex system can be decomposed into several different components via architecture methodology. As a system architecture, you must make clear how to communicate with each other between them.  The other is decision theory, that's mean that architecure is a progress to build a decision-make tree. In another way, that's mean that as a system architecture, you must continously make decision in the whole life cycle of software development. In my viewpoint, I do think both of them are resonable.
